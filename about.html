All About Let's Study!


What is it?

Let’s Study is an web app for organizing study groups, meetings, and anything else!

  • Create/Join a group, add members, and start organizing
  • The interactive map allows for location based study spot recommendations
  • Pick a good time to meet that works for everyone using the chat feature on Discord
  • Don’t forget to check the local weather for the area where you are meeting before you go!
